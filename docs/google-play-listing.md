## Short description

Cute focus timer with manual distraction tracking.

## Full description

Focus Cat Lite is a simple, cute focus timer app for Android.

Choose a focus mode, start a session, and tap **I got distracted** whenever you lose focus. At the end of the session, Focus Cat Lite shows your total focus time and total distractions.

Focus Cat Lite is intentionally simple:

- Local-only and privacy-friendly
- No login
- No ads
- No analytics
- No backend
- No automatic screen wake tracking
- No Accessibility Service
- No UsageStats permission
- No Device Admin

If you want a light, beginner-friendly focus timer that helps you notice distractions, Focus Cat Lite keeps the experience easy to understand.
